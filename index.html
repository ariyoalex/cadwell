Who We Are

A Wholly Indigenous Firm Redefining African Luxury

Incorporated in October 2001, Cadwell Limited was built on a sheer desire to create Africa's most distinguished real estate company. We specialise in developing innovative, unparalleled luxury apartments, residences, and commercial outlets · all located in the most coveted addresses in Lagos, Nigeria.

We understand that luxury is not merely about aesthetics · it is about exclusivity, precision, and an unwavering commitment to our clients. Our customers are our only competition.
